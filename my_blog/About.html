About BLOGS

       

Blogs is an initiative by the Academics and Career council aimed at sharing students' experiences with all. It hosts a wide variety of articles and blogs of students who share their personal experiences when it comes to... well, academics or careers.

   

From various series of articles to how-to guides for internships to project experiences, the Blogs portal covers it all. Get started with the latest content regularly republished on the site.
